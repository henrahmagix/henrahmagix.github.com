Two random images are loaded from picsum.photos. The brightest of the two is overlayed onto the other with a blending mode of "screen". If the difference in average brightness is small, the images are darkened and lightened to make them more distinguishable. Uses CamanJS.

See some of my favourites in this Twitter thread

How to use

Choose a width and height (both default to your screen width) and then click "Generate". Two images will be loaded and then combined. Both images will be shown at half their size, followed by the full-size combined image.

To show of your beautiful and intriguing double exposure, you can click "Save" to download one image comprised of the two individual images at half-size and the full-size combined one.
